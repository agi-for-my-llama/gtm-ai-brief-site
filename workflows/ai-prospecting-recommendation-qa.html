AI Prospecting Recommendation QA

User

SDR leader, RevOps, SalesOps, or founder-led sales team.

Trigger

An AI prospecting agent recommends companies or contacts for outreach.

Inputs

  • ICP definition.
  • Account list.
  • Recommended leads.
  • Source evidence.
  • Persona rules.
  • Disqualification rules.
  • Outreach status.

Output

  • Accepted recommendations.
  • Rejected recommendations.
  • Missing evidence.
  • Persona mismatch list.
  • QA score.
  • Feedback for the agent.

Steps

  1. Sample recommended leads.
  2. Check each account against ICP criteria.
  3. Verify source evidence.
  4. Confirm persona fit and contact relevance.
  5. Reject leads that fail disqualification rules.
  6. Feed rejection reasons back into the prompt or agent configuration.

Prompt

QA these AI prospecting recommendations against the ICP, persona rules, disqualification rules, source evidence, and outreach status. Return accepted leads, rejected leads, missing evidence, persona mismatches, QA score, and recommended changes to the agent instructions.

Success Metric

Accepted recommendations produce better reply or meeting rates than manually selected baseline accounts.
