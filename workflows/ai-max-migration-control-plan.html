AI Max Migration Control Plan

User

Paid search lead, demand gen manager, or agency strategist.

Trigger

Dynamic Search Ads, automatically created assets, or campaign-level broad match settings are scheduled to upgrade to AI Max.

Inputs

  • Campaign list.
  • Spend by campaign.
  • Conversion quality.
  • Landing pages.
  • Search term history.
  • Brand and competitor exclusions.
  • Asset approval rules.

Output

  • Migration risk score.
  • Campaign control checklist.
  • Test campaign selection.
  • Exclusion list.
  • Page gap list.
  • Review cadence.

Steps

  1. Export affected campaigns.
  2. Rank by spend and conversion quality.
  3. Map campaign intent to landing pages.
  4. Mark exclusions and brand constraints.
  5. Identify campaigns that need page cleanup before upgrade.
  6. Pick one low-risk campaign for early testing.
  7. Review search terms, assets, and conversion quality weekly.

Prompt

Create an AI Max migration control plan from these campaigns, spend, landing pages, search terms, exclusions, and conversion metrics. Score migration risk, choose the first test campaign, list controls to preserve, and identify page gaps to fix before upgrade.

Success Metric

No high-spend campaign migrates without exclusions, landing-page fit, and review cadence defined.
