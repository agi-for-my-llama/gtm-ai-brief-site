Lead Routing Approval Loop

User

SalesOps, RevOps, demand gen, or SDR leader.

Trigger

AI recommends routing, ownership, priority, or scoring changes for inbound or outbound leads.

Inputs

  • Lead source.
  • ICP rules.
  • Territory model.
  • Account ownership.
  • Lead score.
  • Disqualification rules.
  • SLA.

Output

  • Routing recommendation.
  • Confidence score.
  • Owner assignment.
  • Approval requirement.
  • Exception queue.
  • SLA timer.

Steps

  1. Define the routing decision the AI can recommend.
  2. Separate auto-approve cases from human-review cases.
  3. Add account ownership and territory checks.
  4. Route low-risk matches automatically.
  5. Send ambiguous matches to an exception queue.
  6. Review acceptance, reassignment, and SLA misses weekly.

Prompt

Design a lead routing approval loop from these lead sources, ICP rules, territory rules, ownership data, scores, disqualification rules, and SLA. Classify cases as auto-route, human-review, or reject. Include confidence thresholds and exception handling.

Success Metric

Lead routing gets faster without increasing reassignment rate or SLA misses.
