Agentic Commerce Offer Readiness

User

Ecommerce lead, lifecycle marketer, paid search manager, or marketplace operator.

Trigger

AI shopping, native checkout, Direct Offers, or agent-assisted buying becomes a meaningful discovery or conversion surface.

Inputs

  • Product category.
  • High-intent shopping questions.
  • Product feed data.
  • Offer rules.
  • Inventory constraints.
  • Landing page.
  • Checkout path.

Output

  • Offer readiness score.
  • Feed gap list.
  • Page gap list.
  • Direct Offer logic.
  • Measurement plan.

Steps

  1. Pick one product category with existing demand.
  2. List five buying questions.
  3. Map each question to product feed attributes and landing page proof.
  4. Define the offer or bundle an agent can explain.
  5. Confirm checkout path and inventory constraints.
  6. Track assisted conversion quality.

Prompt

Evaluate this product category for agentic commerce readiness. Use the buyer questions, product feed, offer rules, landing page, inventory constraints, and checkout path. Identify feed gaps, page gaps, offer logic, and the first test to run.

Success Metric

One product category has clear offer logic, clean feed data, and a checkout path that supports AI-assisted discovery.
