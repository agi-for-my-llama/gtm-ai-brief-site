AI Search Page Refresh

User

Demand gen, content lead, or founder.

Trigger

An AI search result summarizes the category without citing or favorably describing your company.

Inputs

  • Target query.
  • Current AI answer.
  • Top cited pages.
  • Your landing page.
  • Competitor claims.

Output

  • Missing buyer question.
  • Claim gap.
  • Proof gap.
  • Recommended page update.
  • Measurement plan.

Prompt

Compare the AI search answer, cited pages, competitor claims, and our landing page. Identify the missing buyer questions and proof gaps. Recommend concise page updates that improve clarity without making unsupported claims.
