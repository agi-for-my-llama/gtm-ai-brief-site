AI Search Ads Readiness Checklist

Use this before testing AI Mode ads, ChatGPT ads, or AI Max migration.

1. Buyer Questions

  • List 25 converting search terms.
  • List 25 questions from sales calls, demos, chat, or support.
  • Cluster by buyer job: compare, shortlist, troubleshoot, justify, migrate, buy.
  • Pick five questions with commercial intent.

2. Answer Capture

  • Run each question in the AI search or answer surface available to the team.
  • Record the answer.
  • Record cited sources.
  • Record competitor mentions.
  • Mark whether your company is named, ignored, or misdescribed.

3. Landing Page Fit

  • Map each question to one landing page.
  • Check whether the page answers the exact question.
  • Add missing proof, use cases, limitations, integrations, or comparison language.
  • Remove unsupported claims.

4. Policy And Brand Safety

  • Flag regulated, sensitive, or high-stakes language.
  • Confirm claims can be supported by the page.
  • Separate educational answers from promotional claims.
  • Create an approval step before launch.

5. Campaign Controls

  • Define test budget.
  • Define exclusions.
  • Define success metric.
  • Keep core search budget separate.
  • Review AI Max migration settings before September 2026.
