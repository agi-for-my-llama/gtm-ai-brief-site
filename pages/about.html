About AI GTM Fieldbook

AI GTM Fieldbook is a practical workflow library for RevOps and GTM teams turning AI product changes into governed, measurable operating plays.

Most AI coverage explains what launched. This fieldbook focuses on what operators should do next.

What Gets Published

  • Source-backed briefs.
  • Workflow cards.
  • Approval patterns.
  • Measurement templates.
  • GTM operating checklists.

Who It Is For

  • RevOps and SalesOps operators.
  • Demand gen and lifecycle marketers.
  • Founder-led B2B teams.
  • GTM agencies building AI services.

Editorial Standard

Every useful entry should answer:

  • What changed?
  • Who owns the workflow?
  • What triggers it?
  • What inputs are needed?
  • What output should exist?
  • Where does human approval matter?
  • What should be measured?

Current Focus

  • AI search and answer-engine visibility.
  • AI Max and AI search ads readiness.
  • CRM and RevOps governance.
  • Agent ROI and approval workflows.
  • Agentic commerce and offer readiness.
